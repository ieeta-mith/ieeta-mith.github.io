João Rafael Almeida

Principal Investigator (PI)

João Rafael Almeida is an Assistant Professor at DETI and Researcher at IEETA. He currently serves as the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) and Director of the Cybersecurity Office at the University of Aveiro. His research focuses on data privacy, cybersecurity, and handling sensitive data in medical contexts.
